One thing worth flagging, not a grammar issue: **a 30% increase to your biggest customer, announced in a few lines with one month's notice, is a business risk regardless of how polished the wording is.** If you have latitude, consider (a) giving more lead time, (b) a brief justification for the specific number, or (c) offering a call *before* the number lands in writing.
