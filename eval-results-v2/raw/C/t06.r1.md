One thing worth flagging before you send: for your *biggest* customer, a 30% jump attributed only to "rising costs" may land hard. If you have room, two additions tend to soften a hike this size — a **specific reason** (e.g., which costs rose) and an **offer to talk before it takes effect**. I left the draft close to your original, but I'd consider giving this customer a heads-up call rather than leading with email. Want a version with those elements built in?
