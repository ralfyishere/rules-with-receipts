A few shorter alternatives depending on tone:

**Brief and direct**
> Hi [Name] — circling back on [the thing] from last week. Any update on your end? Thanks!

**Warmer**
> Hi [Name], hope your week's going well. Just wanted to gently follow up on [the thing] from last week — let me know if you need anything from me. Thanks!

One suggestion: the original's weak spot is **"the thing from last week."** Naming it specifically (e.g., "the budget proposal," "our chat about the contract") makes it far easier for the recipient to reply quickly, since they won't have to reconstruct what you mean. If you tell me what it's about — and who it's going to — I can tailor it precisely.
